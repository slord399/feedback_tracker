# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR.

## 1. Data We Collect
We collect and process the following data:
- **Guild IDs and Channel IDs:** Stored to provide status tracking and indexing services.
- **User IDs:** Stored when a user indexes a post to track their activity and provide personalized summaries.
- **Message IDs:** Temporarily processed to handle indexing requests and message clean-up.

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing
We process this data based on:
- **Legitimate Interests:** To provide the bot's core functionality as requested by the user or server administrator.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- All data is stored in a private Valkey cluster.
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)
If you are in the UK or EU, you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access:** You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification:** You can request that we correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to Erasure:** You can request that we delete your data.
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** You can request that we limit how we use your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact the bot maintainers via our [Discord Support Server](https://discord.gg/XJHRXwd).

## 5. Data Retention
We retain data as long as the bot is active in your guild or as necessary to provide the services requested. You can remove the bot and request data deletion at any time.

## 6. Third Parties
We do not share your data with third parties, except as required by law or to provide the bot's core functionality (e.g., interacting with Discord API).
